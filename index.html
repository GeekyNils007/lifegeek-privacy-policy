Privacy Policy

LifeGeek AI - Your Privacy Matters
Last updated: January 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LifeGeek AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered fashion assistant application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address and account credentials
  • Profile information (name, preferences, style settings)
  • Fashion preferences and sizing information
  • Device information and usage analytics

Images and Fashion Data

  • Photos you upload for fashion analysis
  • AI-generated style recommendations and analysis results
  • Search history and product interaction data
  • Wardrobe and outfit tracking information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Fashion Analysis: Process uploaded images to provide style recommendations
  • Personalization: Customize recommendations based on your preferences
  • Product Discovery: Search eBay and partner platforms for relevant fashion items
  • Account Management: Maintain your profile, preferences, and service access
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our AI models
  • Communication: Send service updates, tips, and relevant fashion insights

4. Third-Party Integrations

Claude AI by Anthropic

We use Claude AI for advanced fashion analysis and recommendations. Your fashion data is processed according to Anthropic's privacy standards and is not used to train their models.

eBay Product Search

We integrate with eBay's API to show you relevant fashion products. When you interact with eBay product links, you'll be subject to eBay's privacy policy and terms of service.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all communications
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Automated backup and disaster recovery systems

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of data collection at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
  • Fashion Images: Processed locally and deleted after analysis unless saved to wardrobe
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months
  • Legal Compliance: As required by applicable laws and regulations

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session and preferences
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns (with your consent)
  • Provide personalized fashion recommendations

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our privacy dashboard.

9. Children's Privacy

LifeGeek AI is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately to have it removed.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our services after such modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
